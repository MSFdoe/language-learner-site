Why a terminal tool?

Your data stays on your computer.

No account. No cloud. No company holding your vocabulary hostage. Your database is a file on your machine. Back it up, move it, keep it forever.

Unlimited storage.

Language apps give you what fits on their servers. A terminal tool gives you what fits on your hard drive — which is thousands of times more. Learn 50 words or 50,000. There is no premium tier.

It's faster.

No loading screens. No animations. No popups asking you to rate the app. Type a number, press Enter. That's it.

You own the tool.

It runs on your machine. It works offline (except translations). No subscription that expires. No company that can shut down and take your progress with it.

One command to install.

If you can copy and paste, you can install it. The terminal is not scary — it's just typing. And once you try it, you'll wonder why you ever clicked buttons.
