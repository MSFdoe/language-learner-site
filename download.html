Setup (2 minutes)

1

Get a free DeepL API key

Go to deepl.com/pro#developer, sign up for the free plan (500,000 characters/month), and copy your API key.

2

Open your terminal

Mac: Open "Terminal" (search for it in Spotlight).
Windows: Open "Command Prompt" or "PowerShell".
Linux: You already know.

3

Make it runnable (Mac/Linux only)

After downloading, run this once:

chmod +x ~/Downloads/language_learner-macos-arm64

(Replace the filename with whichever version you downloaded)

4

Run it

~/Downloads/language_learner-macos-arm64

It will ask for your DeepL key, let you pick 3 languages, and you're ready to go.

5

Optional: Move it to your PATH

So you can run it from anywhere by just typing language_learner:

sudo mv ~/Downloads/language_learner-macos-arm64 /usr/local/bin/language_learner
Your data is yours. Everything is stored on your computer in ~/.language_learner/. Your vocabulary database grows with every session and never expires. Back it up like any other file.
